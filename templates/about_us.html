About Us

Welcome to our Indian Sign Language (ISL) Detection Project! This initiative aims to bridge communication gaps for the deaf and hard-of-hearing community by developing an intelligent system that can detect and translate ISL gestures into text and speech.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create an innovative solution that empowers individuals who use ISL for communication. By combining state-of-the-art machine learning, computer vision, and speech synthesis technologies, we aim to provide real-time, seamless translation of ISL into a form that can be understood by everyone.

Technology Used

  • Indian Sign Language Detection: Advanced machine learning models for gesture-to-text translation.
  • Speech Synthesis: Using pyttsx3 for seamless text-to-speech conversion.
  • Computer Vision: Real-time video feed processing via OpenCV and Mediapipe.
  • Web Interface: Flask-powered user-friendly interface for intuitive interaction.
